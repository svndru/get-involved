Fundraising Ideas

There are ways to participate in our mission, blah blah blah.

“Paw-sitive Prints”
Sticker and Keychain Sale
  • Small items, big impact! Each sticker and keychain is inspired by rescued animals’ unique stories.
  • By buying one, you’re not just adding flair to your bag—you’re leaving a paw-print on their future.
“Pet-tition For Hope”
Freedom wall
  • Donate and leave a love note!
  • Each message of encouragement—real or virtual—becomes a beacon of hope on our “Pet-tition for Hope” wall, showing rescued pets they’re not forgotten. Even animals can feel love when it's written from the heart.
“Box for Paws”
Relief drive
  • We’re collecting food packs and pet essentials to create evenly distributed care boxes for strays and shelter animals. Whether it’s a scoop of kibble, a can of food, or a treat—every donation helps fill a hungry bowl and wag a lonely tail.
  • Donate what you can. We’ll pack it with love.
“P.U.R.R. FUNd”
Pledge. Unite. Rescue. Rebuild.
  • This isn’t just a donation—it’s a movement. Join the P.U.R.R. FUNd by pledging your heart to unite communities, rescue strays, and rebuild lives.
  • Every act of support helps turn pain into healing, and loneliness into love. Together, we rescue with purpose and rebuild with hope.
"Leash of Love"
Wear and Care campaign
Bracelet and phone straps related/inspired by pet leashes
  • More than just accessories, these are leashes of love. Inspired by the strength of real pet leashes, each handmade bracelet or strap represents rescue, recovery, and hope.
  • The colors speak in silent messages—and every purchase leads a stray one step closer to home.
